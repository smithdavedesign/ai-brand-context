# Generative AI Rules

## Hard Rules (No Exceptions)
- **NEVER** incorporate any element of our logo into any generative AI image
- **NEVER** incorporate the Solidigm name into any generative AI image
- **NEVER** use gen AI to develop a logo, lockup, icon, or any visual meant to "brand" a program, team, resource, event, or document
- All external-facing generative AI imagery **requires review and approval** by the Brand and Creative teams before use

## Required Disclaimer
Always add: **"AI-generated"** or **"Generated with Copilot"** (or whichever platform was used). The Copilot icon alone is not sufficient.

## Usage Guidelines
- Avoid overly generic, busy, tropey, or cartoony images
- Avoid AI-generated humans or animals
- Avoid anything requiring precision or accuracy (SSDs, internal components)
- If using purple in AI images, ensure it is one of our brand purples
- AI images complement a concept; they do not replace detailed graphic design work
- Only use AI tools as permitted in the Corporate AI Usage and Development Information Security Policy

## Approval Process
Submit to the Brand and Creative teams via the AI imagery approval form. Reviewed in the Gen AI Review meeting every other week.
