# Solidigm Brand Foundation

## Who We Are
Solidigm (思得) — a combination of *solid* and *paradigm*. We are setting the new standard for the storage industry. Formed from SK hynix's acquisition of Intel's NAND and SSD business in 2021.

**Mission:** Unlock data's virtually unlimited potential through advancements in storage technology. We see current limitations as future opportunities.

**Goal:** Become the #1 NAND business by 2030.

## Our Values
- **Customer Inspired** — Listen, understand, anticipate, and partner to solve problems. Honor commitments above all.
- **Invention** — Passionate inventors and technologists unlocking the power of data to fuel human advancement.
- **Excellence** — High-quality, responsible technologies with tenacity and integrity.
- **Openness** — Equitable, respectful, valuing diverse perspectives. Diversity drives innovation.
- **Agility** — Act boldly and with urgency. Nimble, responsive, willing to take intelligent risks.
- **Partnership** — Relationships with teammates, customers, partners, and communities come first.

## Brand Personality: The Seasoned Challenger
Thought leaders with a thoughtful outlook for partners, employees, the environment, and the world. We relish challenges and are inspired by unsolved problems.

## Brand Tone & Voice
- **Optimistic** — Upbeat and enthusiastic devotees of possibility thinking (but not unrealistic day-dreamers)
- **Analytical** — Thoughtful, intelligent and deliberate (but not robotic or staid)
- **Passionate** — Spirited and energized by technology, excited to engage (but not argumentative or confrontational)
- **Genuine** — Authentic, honest and straightforward, human and dynamic (but not trivial or unprepared)
- **Ambitious** — Confident and determined, appreciative of hard work (but not arrogant or dismissive)
