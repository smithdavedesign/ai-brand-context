# Do Nots (Hard Constraints)

These are absolute constraints for any AI-generated output. Violating these risks brand damage, trademark issues, or accessibility failures.

## Identity & Trademark
- ❌ Never generate or alter the Solidigm logo in any way
- ❌ Never use the ® symbol — use ™ only
- ❌ Never use © in association with the name or logo
- ❌ Never incorporate Solidigm's name or logo into AI-generated imagery
- ❌ Never create a bespoke logo for a team, program, or event — use department lockup guidelines

## Color
- ❌ Never use accent colors (Electric Teal, Pink, Orange) as dominant or primary design elements
- ❌ Never use Electric Teal or Electric Pink outside of approved creative design contexts
- ❌ Never use chart-specific colors (Medium Blue, Dark Teal) outside of charts/graphs
- ❌ Never use high-contrast gradients mixing multiple hues
- ❌ Never "purple wash" photography
- ❌ Never break WCAG 2.1 AA contrast standards (minimum 4.5:1)
- ❌ Never use the white logo on light backgrounds or the purple logo on dark backgrounds

## Typography
- ❌ Never set headlines or subheads in all uppercase
- ❌ Never use bold weight mid-sentence to emphasize individual words
- ❌ Never use a font other than Sora (non-Latin), Noto Sans (character languages), or Avenir Next LT Pro (Microsoft Office)
- ❌ Never center-align large blocks of text in horizontal layouts
- ❌ Never use the possessive form of Solidigm when referring to the brand (only the company)

## Logo Usage
- ❌ Never alter, distort, stretch, or recolor the logo
- ❌ Never add drop shadows or heavy effects to the logo
- ❌ Never have the Solidigm logo appear second or smaller in co-marketing
- ❌ Never use the stacked (space-constrained) logo without brand approval
- ❌ Never use the brand mark entirely alone without prior brand approval

## Generative AI
- ❌ Never use gen AI to create logos, lockups, icons, or branded visual elements
- ❌ Never publish external AI-generated imagery without Brand and Creative team approval
- ❌ Never omit the "AI-generated" disclaimer from AI imagery

## Iconography
- ❌ Never use gradients in icons
- ❌ Never use more than one line weight in an icon
- ❌ Never use icons inline with text
- ❌ Never display icons larger than 200px without brand approval
