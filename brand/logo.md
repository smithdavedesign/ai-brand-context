# Logo

## Logo Construction
The Solidigm logo consists of two parts:
- **Brand Mark** — the "S" shape derived from interlocking solid-state drive shapes
- **Word Mark** — "Solidigm" in Sora typeface

The logo DNA is built on **45° and 90° angles** — representing precision, quality, and partnership. The interlocking shapes symbolize our commitment to partnership.

## Logo Versions
1. **Primary Logo** — Brand mark + word mark on a single horizontal line (default, preferred)
2. **Separated Logo** — Brand mark and word mark separated, sharing left or right alignment. 66% minimum height of layout must lie between them. Works best with generous vertical space.
3. **Space-Constrained (Stacked) Logo** — Stacked version for horizontal space constraints, partner logo lockups, or "logo soup" situations. **Requires brand approval before use** — contact brand@solidigm.com
4. **Brand Mark Alone** — Only when the full logo lockup is already visible on the same page/screen

## Logo Color Versions
- **Primary Color (Purple)** — Use on light backgrounds (white, tan, light gray) only
- **White** — Use on dark backgrounds; optically adjusted for dark use — never use on light backgrounds
- **High Contrast (Black)** — Use sparingly, only on medium gray backgrounds where purple or white would fail

## Clear Space
- Minimum clear space around logo = width of the "M" in the word mark
- When aligning to layouts, align to the "M" in Solidigm, not to the TM notation

## Minimum Sizes
- Primary logo: 50px (screen) / 13mm / 0.5 inch (print)
- Remove TM when smaller than 72px or 0.75 inch (primary), 48px or 0.5 inch (brand mark alone or stacked)

## Trademark Placement Within Logo
- TM symbol is always placed **bottom right** of each logo configuration
- Do not move or resize the TM notation within provided logo files
- Do not add or change spacing between TM notation and logo

## Co-Marketing / Partnership Lockups
- Solidigm logo and partner logo should feel **visually balanced** — equal ownership
- Use a **plus sign (+) in Gray** between logos (or light gray for contrast)
- Horizontal lockups preferred; vertical pairings available when space demands
- Solidigm logo must **never appear second or smaller** in co-marketing
- Do not lock up the Solidigm logo with any words or phrases

## Logo Misuse (Never Do These)
- Do not alter the color of the logo — use only approved colors
- Do not add drop shadows or other heavy effects
- Do not distort or stretch
- Do not alter the "S" mark size or position
- Do not create a condensed version
- Do not place white logo on light backgrounds or purple logo on dark backgrounds
- Do not use accent colors (teal, pink, orange) for the logo or as logo background
- Do not have Solidigm logo appear second or smaller in co-marketing
