# Platform Overrides: Marketing

Applies to: marketing landing pages, campaign pages, email templates, social media assets.

## Typography
- Headlines: Sora Thin (200), title case
- Body: Sora Light (300) or Regular (400)
- CTAs: Sora SemiBold (600), uppercase, 2px letter-spacing

## Color Priorities
- Lead with Solidigm Purple as hero/dominant color
- Dark Blue for overlays and section backgrounds
- Accent colors permitted as small highlights only (buttons, underlines, badges)
- Electric Teal and Electric Pink require brand approval before use in marketing materials

## Photography
- Level 1 retouching only (standard)
- Level 2 allowed for hero images with Creative team sign-off
- Documentary style preferred; never overly staged

## Generative AI
- All AI-generated imagery in external marketing requires Brand and Creative team approval
- Must include "AI-generated" disclaimer
- Never use AI for product renders, logos, or icons

## Layout
- Left-align body text; center-align only for short hero headlines in vertical layouts
- Brand Angle graphic element permitted as background element
- Logo must appear in primary (purple) version on light backgrounds, white on dark
