# Name & Trademark

## Correct Usage
- Always write as **Solidigm** — no modifications or augmentations
- Use **TM** (™) superscript on first and/or most prominent instance: **Solidigm™**
- After first use, the trademark symbol can be dropped for subsequent mentions in the same document
- **Never use ®** (registered trademark) — not yet registered as of Jan 2026
- **Never use ©** in association with the name or logo

## Possessive Form
- Solidigm **can** be used in possessive form when referring to the company (e.g., "Solidigm's engineering team")
- Solidigm **should NOT** be used in possessive form when referring to the brand
- When in doubt, rephrase to avoid possessive — contact brand@Solidigm.com

## Product Naming Rules
- Use product names **exactly as written** — no modifications, including capitalization
- Do NOT use product name without series name (e.g., never "The P5520" — use the full product name with series)
- Do NOT use possessive form of Solidigm before product name (never "Solidigm's D5-P5336")
- No longer use "(formerly Intel®)" — that transition period has ended

## Chinese Name
- Chinese name: **思得** (Sī Dé / "See Duh")
- First mention in text: **Solidigm™ (思得)**
- Subsequent mentions in same document: can use 思得 alone
- Solidigm written in Sora or Avenir Next LT Pro; 思得 always written in **Noto Sans**
