## Color Usage Rules
- **Lead with Solidigm Purple** as the dominant brand color wherever possible
- Use Ultra Dark Purple and Dark Blue for background elements or overlays
- Use accent colors (Teal, Pink, Orange) **only as accents or highlights** — never dominant
- Electric colors (Teal, Pink) for **creative design only** — require brand approval
- Never use accent colors in dominant situations (e.g., as the Brand Angle fill color)
- Never use high-contrast gradients — any gradient must use tints or shades of a single hue
- Do not "purple wash" photos or set full headlines in accent colors

## Approved Color Pairings (Logo & UI)
- White logo on Dark Blue, Dark Purple, Dark Gray ✓
- Purple logo on White, Light Gray ✓
- Purple logo or Dark Blue logo on Medium Gray (high contrast version only) ✓
- Do NOT use Purple on Gray — use Dark Blue instead
- Do NOT use Purple on Light Purple — use White instead
- Do NOT use Purple on Dark Blue/Dark Purple — use White or Light Purple instead

## Color Accessibility
All brand colors pass **WCAG 2.1 AA** (minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio). Always maintain this standard. See approved pairings in the guidelines for compliant combinations.
